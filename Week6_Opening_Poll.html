Changing Gender Norms: What Do You Think — and What Does the Data Show?

Instructions

This short poll asks for your views on changing gender norms — and invites you to compare them with findings from recent global surveys.

For each question, select your answer and click Submit. You will immediately see how your response compares to data from surveys such as the Ipsos International Women's Day Report, the WEF Global Gender Gap Report, the UNDP Gender Social Norms Index, and research from Stanford and Equimundo.

There are no right or wrong answers on opinion questions. On estimation questions, see how close you get to the actual global figures.

Your responses are not collected or stored.
